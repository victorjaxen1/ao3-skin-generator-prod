📋 Content Policy

Guidelines for responsible and ethical use of AO3 Skin Generator

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Our Mission: AO3 Skin Generator exists to help fanfiction writers create immersive social media AU (Alternate Universe) stories. We believe in the power of transformative fiction while recognizing our responsibility to prevent misuse.

🎭 This Tool is for Fiction Only

AO3 Skin Generator is designed exclusively for:

  • Fanfiction: Creative stories published on Archive of Our Own (AO3) and similar platforms
  • Parody: Satirical or comedic content that is clearly fictional
  • Roleplay: Collaborative storytelling between consenting participants
  • Educational: Demonstrating how social media interfaces work

✅ Good Use Example: Creating a Twitter thread for your OTP (One True Pairing) in a coffee shop AU where fictional characters have a cute exchange.

🚫 Prohibited Uses

The following uses of AO3 Skin Generator are strictly prohibited:

1. Disinformation & Fake News

  • Creating fake tweets, messages, or posts designed to be mistaken for real content
  • Fabricating statements by public figures, celebrities, or politicians
  • Spreading false information about real events, people, or organizations
  • Creating content intended to manipulate public opinion or elections

2. Impersonation & Fraud

  • Impersonating real people without clear fictional framing
  • Creating fake verified accounts to deceive viewers
  • Using generated content for scams, phishing, or social engineering
  • Pretending generated content is real for any deceptive purpose

3. Harassment & Harm

  • Creating fake conversations to harass, bully, or defame individuals
  • Fabricating evidence for use in disputes or legal matters
  • Creating content that incites violence or hatred
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery (even if fictional)

4. Criminal Activity

  • Evidence tampering or fabrication
  • Blackmail or extortion using fake content
  • Any use that violates local, state, national, or international law

⚠️ Legal Warning: Creating fake social media content that damages someone's reputation may constitute defamation. Creating fake content to influence elections may violate election laws. We cooperate with law enforcement when required by law.

🏷️ The "PARODY" Label

To prevent misuse, all verified badges generated by this tool include a "PARODY" label that cannot be removed within the tool. This label:

  • Clearly identifies the content as fictional/satirical
  • Protects you from accusations of impersonation
  • Helps readers understand the content is not real
  • Is required for responsible use of the verified badge feature

Deliberately removing the PARODY label (via external editing tools) and presenting the content as real is a violation of our Terms of Service and may expose you to legal liability.

✅ Best Practices for Fanfiction

When using AO3 Skin Generator for your stories:

  1. Use fictional characters or clearly fictional versions of real people (RPF with appropriate tags)
  2. Tag appropriately on AO3: Use tags like "Social Media AU", "Fake Tweets", "Twitter Fic Format"
  3. Include author's notes clarifying that all social media content is fictional
  4. Don't share out of context: If sharing screenshots on social media, clearly label them as fiction
  5. Respect real people: Even in RPF, avoid content that could genuinely harm someone's reputation

🔍 How We Enforce This Policy

While we cannot monitor all uses of generated content (it runs in your browser), we:

  • Include the PARODY label on verified badges by default
  • Add watermarks to exported images (removable, but present by default)
  • Reserve the right to modify the tool to prevent misuse
  • Cooperate with legal authorities when presented with valid legal requests
  • May terminate access for users who violate this policy

📢 Reporting Misuse

If you encounter content created with AO3 Skin Generator being used to spread disinformation or harm individuals:

  1. Report the content to the platform where it's being shared (Twitter, Facebook, etc.)
  2. Contact us at hello@wordfokus.com with details
  3. If it involves illegal activity, report to appropriate authorities

🤝 Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Supporting the fanfiction community's creative expression
  • Continuously improving safeguards against misuse
  • Listening to community feedback on policy improvements
  • Being transparent about how we handle policy violations

Questions? If you're unsure whether your intended use is appropriate, please contact us at hello@wordfokus.com. We're happy to help!
