**Background:** Neutral (white, light gray, or subtle gradient)  
**Framing:** Head and shoulders portrait, centered  
**Lighting:** Soft, even lighting from front  
**Focus:** Sharp focus on face, slight blur on background

## Post-Processing Tips

### For ALL avatars:
1. **Crop to square** (1:1 ratio)
2. **Center the face** in frame
3. **Check skin tones** render accurately across diversity
4. **Optimize file size** to ~200-500KB
5. **Use consistent backgrounds** across similar categories
6. **Test at small size** (100x100px) to ensure recognizability
7. **Ensure no copyright issues** - original AI-generated only

### Quality Control Checklist:
- [ ] Face is clearly visible and centered
- [ ] Expression matches character archetype
- [ ] Clothing appropriate for character type
- [ ] Background doesn't distract from face
- [ ] Lighting flatters the character
- [ ] No artifacts or distortions
- [ ] Character looks approachable and relatable
- [ ] Passes diversity and representation standards
